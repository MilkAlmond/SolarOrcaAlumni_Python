Frequently Asked Questions

1. I can't create an account. What should I do?
Check that you are using a valid student ID and entered the correct graduation year. If the problem persists, contact the alumni office.
2. I forgot my student ID. How can I get it?
Contact the alumni office at +86 123 4567 8901 (8am-12pm or 2pm-5pm weekdays) or email support@solarorca.edu.
3. I graduated but my graduation year isn't recognized.
Make sure you are entering the year you actually completed your degree, not the year you started. If you graduated before 1990, please contact support for manual verification.
4. I have multiple degrees. Which graduation year do I enter?
Enter the graduation year for your first/the earliest degree (e.g., your Bachelor's). Once logged in, you can add your other degrees (Master's, PhD, etc.) to your profile.
5. How do I verify my alumni status?
Enter your Student ID and Graduation Year on the signup form. The system checks these against alumni records. Once matched, your account is verified immediately.
6. My signup page looks weird. Help!
Please use a PC (desktop or laptop) to sign up. Our website has not yet been optimized for mobile devices.
7. What browsers are supported?
Our website works best with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.
8. How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions in the reset link sent to your email.
9. Can current students use this system?
Yes, current students can browse public threads and view class information, but they cannot post until they graduate.
10. How do I update my profile information?
Log in and go to "My Profile" → "Edit Profile". You can update your name, email, job information, academic history, and work history.
11. What happens if I forget to verify my email?
You will not be able to access alumni-only features until you complete email verification. The verification code expires in 10 minutes, but you can request a new one.
12. Is my personal information safe?
Yes, we take privacy seriously. You control who can see your profile, email, and phone number in the Privacy Settings.
13. How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the "Report" button under any thread or reply. Select a reason and submit. An admin will review your report.
14. Can I delete my account?
Yes, go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
15. Who do I contact for technical support?
Email support@solarorca.edu or call +86 123 4567 8901 during business hours (8am-12pm, 2pm-5pm weekdays).
16. Why can't I see certain threads?
Some threads are marked "Alumni Only" and are only visible to verified alumni. If you are an alumnus, make sure your account is verified.
